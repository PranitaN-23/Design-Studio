About Our Studio

The story, passion, and process behind our designs.

At Design Studio by Komal, we believe every space tells a story. Our mission is to transform homes and offices into environments that blend comfort, beauty, and functionality.

With years of experience in interior design, we have curated a wide range of modern and traditional styles, delivering spaces that reflect the personalities of the people who live and work in them.

Our Process

1. Consultation

We start by listening to your vision, needs, and budget to understand your perfect space.

2. Design & Planning

We create detailed mood boards and 3D renderings to bring your vision to life on paper.

3. Execution & Styling

We manage the project from start to finish, ensuring a flawless execution and a beautiful result.
